What it does

A focused workspace for serious LinkedIn publishers.

No vanity dashboards, no auto-engagement, no "growth hacks." Just the few things that matter when you publish for yourself and the company pages you represent.

Compose & schedule

Write posts in a clean editor, save drafts, and schedule them for the moments your audience is actually on LinkedIn. Edit, reschedule, or cancel any time before publish.

Multi-actor publishing

Post as yourself, or on behalf of the LinkedIn Pages you administer. Switch actors with a single click — the active identity is always visible, so nothing goes out from the wrong account.

Reshare with intro

Amplify posts from your team, your company, or your network — with an optional intro paragraph on top. Attribution and the original author stay intact.

Monitor curated pages

Follow a hand-picked list of LinkedIn pages and creators in one feed. Spot what's worth resharing or replying to, without falling into the algorithmic timeline.

LinkedIn API usage

How we use the LinkedIn API.

This page exists in part for the LinkedIn Developer Platform review. Below is the exact, plain-language list of LinkedIn APIs and scopes PostmateAI requests, and what each one does in the product.

openid · profile · email Sign In with LinkedIn OpenID Connect

Authenticate tool users

We use Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect as our only sign-in method. When a user signs in, we receive their LinkedIn member ID, name, profile picture, and verified email address.

That's it — we don't ask for connections, messages, search, or any data about people the user knows.

w_member_social Share on LinkedIn Standard product

Publish posts as the authenticated user

With the user's consent, PostmateAI publishes posts to LinkedIn on their behalf — only the posts they explicitly drafted and scheduled inside the tool. Each post passes through the standard Share on LinkedIn endpoint with the content, media, and visibility the user selected.

No post is ever published without an explicit user action (clicking Publish or scheduling a specific time).

w_organization_social rw_organization_admin Marketing Developer Platform

Publish on behalf of LinkedIn Pages the user administers

For users who administer one or more LinkedIn Pages, we request the Marketing Developer Platform scopes so the user can:

  • See the list of Pages they are an admin of, surfaced in PostmateAI's actor switcher.
  • Publish posts to those Pages, with the same explicit-action rule as personal posts.
  • Reshare existing posts from those Pages with an optional intro paragraph.

We do not modify Page settings, change admins, or perform any administrative action other than reading the admin list and publishing the content the user authored.

What PostmateAI does not do

  • No scraping of LinkedIn or any third-party site.
  • No sale or sharing of user data with third parties.
  • No automated likes, comments, follows, or DMs.
  • No engagement beyond what the user explicitly schedules.
Data & privacy

Tokens stay encrypted. Disconnect any time.

OAuth access and refresh tokens issued by LinkedIn are stored encrypted at rest and used only to perform the actions a signed-in user has explicitly taken inside PostmateAI. We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties, and there are no advertising or tracking integrations on this site.

Users can disconnect their LinkedIn account at any time from inside the app, which revokes our tokens and deletes the associated profile and Page data from our systems. Full details are in the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
